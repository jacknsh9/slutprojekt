BMW M5 CS

The Fastest Production BMW Sedan

The BMW M5 CS represents the peak of high-performance luxury sedans. It combines everyday practicality with supercar-level performance. As the most powerful production BMW at the time of its release, the M5 CS elevated the M5 legacy to new heights.

Power Meets Precision

>Powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 producing 627 horsepower, the M5 CS can accelerate from 0–60 mph in under 3 seconds. BMW’s advanced all-wheel-drive system ensures maximum traction while maintaining rear-wheel-drive character when desired. Despite its size and luxury features, weight-saving measures such as carbon fiber components improved agility and responsiveness.

The BMW M5 CS uses the latest version of BMW’s S63 4.4-litre twin-turbo V8, featuring a “hot-vee” turbo layout for better response and efficiency. The S63 was introduced in the F10-generation BMW M5 as a replacement for the older naturally aspirated V10, and it went on to power models like the BMW M6 and later the BMW M8. Over time, the engine evolved through several updates, increasing power and refinement, with the M5 CS representing its most extreme and final form.

Modern Motorsport Spirit

The M5 CS proves that motorsport heritage can coexist with advanced technology and comfort. It is a perfect example of how BMW M continues to evolve — blending raw power with innovation and refinement.
